Pill Care's

Pivoting Page


This page describes the pivots that we have already undertaken throughout the quarter, any pivots that we plan on doing now, and any pivots we believe would be necessary going forward if we are to continue to work on our startup after ECE 140B.A pivot means fundamentally changing the direction of a business when you realize the current products or services aren't meeting the needs of the market. The main goal of a pivot is to help a company improve revenue or survive in the market, but the way you pivot your business can make all the difference.



Pivots We Took

Customer-Need Pivot


  We still focus on elders but we find that elders taking pills on time is a more serious and clear problem compared to wearable watches that detect unprecedented falling. The need is larger and more inevitable, and the customer is easier to accept the product of pill-taking reminders compared to a watch that requires constant wearing with a GPS location feature.
Original: Wearable Watch that detects falling for elders
Now: a box that reminds elders to take pills on time with the correct dosage with the care of family members.
Pivots in Future

Zoom-out Pivot


Now: a box that reminds elders to take pills on time with the correct dosage with the care of family members.
Future: Auto pill dispenser that will not only remind elders to take pills on time with the correct dosage but also display family membersâ€™ messages and photos they upload for elders when taking pills on time.



Business Architecture Pivot


Now: we sell our product directly to the customers (B2C) for those who want a reliable and caring pill reminder for the elders in the family.
Future: we will shift to B2B: we will sell our product to major pharmaceutical companies, such as CVS and Walgreens. As B2B strategies have a higher volume in sales, this is beneficial for business development and brand foundation.
