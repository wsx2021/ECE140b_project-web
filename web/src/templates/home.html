Description (Tentative):

Stay Awake is an App on phones and smartwatches that helps drivers detect driving fatigue and alarm them to prevent car accidents happening. Stay Awake utilizes phone embedded camera and optional smartwatches’ biosensor data to detect driving fatigue in multiple approaches including basic eye closeness detection and distraction detection based on computer vision and heart attack, high blood pressure flow based on optional biosensor on smartwatches. Stay Awake also records data of the drivers under the consent of cooperating companies to further protect the drivers and maximize the efficiency and safety of drivers. The major unfair advantage is our cheap price compared to any existing IoT-based camera mount solution because we don’t require extra hardware to experience the product.
